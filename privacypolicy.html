Privacy Policy

This policy explains how The Simplest Calorie Tracker handles data when you use the app.

Effective date: March 24, 2026 Applies to: The Simplest Calorie Tracker iOS app

Summary

The Simplest Calorie Tracker is designed to work without creating an account. Your nutrition entries are stored on your device. We do not sell your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect the information you enter in the app, such as foods, calories, and goals. This data stays on your device.

If you contact support, we receive your email address and the content of your message so we can respond.

How We Use Information

  • To provide app features like meal logging and goal tracking.
  • To respond to support requests and improve reliability.

Data Storage and Retention

Your entries are stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud device backups, your data may be included in those backups and managed by Apple.

Support emails are retained only as long as needed to resolve your request.

Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties except to provide support communications.

Your Choices

  • You can delete entries within the app at any time.
  • You can remove all app data by deleting the app from your device.
  • You can request help by emailing khayet.paul@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

The Simplest Calorie Tracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we will update this page with a new effective date.

Questions about privacy? Contact khayet.paul@gmail.com.
