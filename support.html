Before You Email

To help us troubleshoot faster, include:

Quick Answers

Common questions from new users:

  • Most features work offline; your entries stay on your device.
  • If you reinstall the app, data may be restored from your iCloud device backup.
  • You can request data deletion by removing entries in the app or deleting the app.
